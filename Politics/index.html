Day One: A New Era Begins

Radical transparency, global cooperation, and the end of artificial scarcity through technology and human purpose.

Complete Transparency

From Day One, I will stream my activities on GoPro and report exactly what I am doing at all times. No closed doors, no secret meetings, no hidden agendas. The American people deserve to see their president's every decision in real-time.

01

Human-Centered Foreign Policy

We must talk to everyone as a person, and every nation as people. No more faceless entities - we will appreciate their cultures and work to bring better education, healthcare, and technology access to all. Moving away from warlike tendencies toward global cooperation.

02

Weapons to Prosperity

Repurpose nuclear weapons for nuclear energy, transform warheads into planters for food production. The age of arms races is over - humanity can create thinking machines smarter than any human who has ever lived. It's time to beat swords into plowshares.

03

Border-Free Collaboration

A world where governments maintain their areas with unique beliefs and cultures, but people are free to move, learn, and experience different societies. Equal access to medicine and technology everywhere - no more artificial scarcity.

The End of Artificial Scarcity

Technology has proven time and again that resources gain better use through innovation. We must eliminate the middleman and help each other as a planet. Supply chains will be modified for efficiency and safety - reducing monocropping, creating efficient farms, clean energy sources, and better housing systems.

"Humans don't need to be deprived of food and medicine to continue working. We must provide education and allow each person to follow their goals, work in their preferred fields, and contribute to something larger than themselves."

Competition will drive innovation through additional novelties - newer technologies, better solutions - but driven by the innate human want to be useful, to have purpose, to be part of the power process that fulfills our deepest needs.

For Every American

Common sense solutions that transcend party lines - see how science-based policies deliver what you actually care about.

Environmental Protection & Social Justice

Science-based policies that protect our planet and ensure equality for all Americans.

Environmental Protection

Protecting our national forests, state parks, and coastlines. Keeping our beaches and ecosystems pristine through science-based conservation policies.

Green Energy Revolution

Massive investment in truly green energy systems and nuclear power. Divesting from fossil fuels and dramatically lowering emissions through technological innovation.

Hate Crime Prosecution

Swift, fair prosecution of anyone who commits acts against others based on race, sexual orientation, creed, or any form of hatred. Evil will not be tolerated.

Common Sense Courts

Justice systems where punishment fits the crime. Someone stealing bread to eat shouldn't be treated the same as someone stealing a $25 watch. Proportional, rational justice.

Constitutional Rights & Traditional Values

Protecting fundamental freedoms while respecting faith and personal responsibility.

Theological Studies in Schools

Optional theological studies alongside philosophy. Christianity, Islam, Judaism, and all belief systems can be practiced openly. Students learn wisdom from the Bible, Sun Tzu, Bhagavad Gita, and social contract theory.

Gun Rights Maintained

Constitutional rights protected. Criminals won't follow gun laws anyway - law-abiding citizens deserve the right to defend themselves. The law should back your right to protection.

Hunting & Fishing Rights

Hunting lands and tag systems maintained but modernized for conservation. More money spent on efficient, smart, and fair systems that increase game availability while protecting ecosystems.

Government Efficiency

DOGE-style efficiency board with scientists and experts (not random people). Streamline government while maintaining core services during transition. Decisions based on what's best for humanity, not political agendas.

Common Sense Solutions

Beyond party politics - practical, science-based policies that actually work for real people.

Evidence-Based Policy

All decisions backed by science and data, not political expediency. Policies that actually work based on evidence, not ideology or party lines.

Individual Freedom

Maximum personal liberty within a framework of mutual respect. Freedom of religion, self-defense, and personal choice - balanced with responsibility to others.

Fiscal Responsibility

Efficient government spending with scientific oversight. Cut waste, not essential services. Invest in what actually improves lives and drives progress.

Practical Solutions

Solutions that actually work in the real world. No more empty promises or ideological extremes - just effective policies that improve everyday American lives.
